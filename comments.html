Comments: Collaboration in Subscription Forms and Supporting Documents

2021 – Present

Of all the projects I have been involved in while working on Fund Subscription, I must say that working on comments has been my favorite. It was a lot of fun, but it definitely came with its challenges, especially when it came to building something completely new from scratch.

My team and I got to imagine the collaboration experience and build the feature from the ground up to streamline communication between fund managers and investors, making it easier for them to work together on subscription forms and supporting documents without having to leave the app. With comments, we successfully reduced the need for offline and email exchanges between users, making their process more efficient and user-friendly.
