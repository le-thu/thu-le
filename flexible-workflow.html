About the product

Anduin Fund Subscription streamlines the fund subscription process for fund managers and their investors. With our platform, fund managers can easily invite investors to fill out required documents, upload AML/KYC documents, and sign their subscription documents using our built-in e-signature tool or by uploading signature pages, all in one convenient location. Our dashboard provides a birds-eye view of where investors are in the process, allowing fund managers to monitor fundraising performance and assist investors with any questions or tasks related to the subscription. Meanwhile, investors have their own user interface to track their subscription progress, view remaining form fields, upload additional documents, and sign side letters.
