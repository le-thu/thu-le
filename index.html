About

I'm a self-taught designer who finds joy in crafting meaningful, simple digital experiences through a goal-driven and data-informed approach, and I love that I get to do this by being a collaborator working with many folks from product and engineering to customer success.

I spent the first half of my life in Vietnam and the other half in the States, and now I'm based in sunny Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City), Vietnam — my birth city and one that holds a special place in my heart.

I could say I stumbled upon design when I started to tinker with photography and filmmaking a decade ago, which I always give credit to for my passion for design and user experience. To me, they're merely different mediums used to tell a story, a message, a connection.
