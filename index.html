About

I'm a self-taught designer who finds joy in crafting interface design and facilitating cross-functional collaboration. I spent the first half of my life in Vietnam, the other half in Florida, and now I'm here in the West Coast.

Previously, I helped lead design efforts for identity and product marketing at fintech startup Liquid, and later on helped design several eCommerce projects for B2B clients as the sole designer at SAI Digital.

When I'm not working, I'm probably reading news on tech and business, catching up with my friends and network from the other side of the world, and keeping up with my weekly journal. I credit my passion for design and user experience to photography and filmmaking as they're merely different mediums with different tools used to — more importantly — tell a story, a message, a connection.
