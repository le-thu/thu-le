About

For the last 5 years, I've been crafting purposeful user experiences across a range of industries. My approach revolves around using user research and data to inform my decision-making process, taking into account the needs of both end-users and business objectives. I take pride in finding the right balance between form and function.

Currently, I call Saigon, Vietnam my home base. This is where I was born and spent the first half of my life before venturing off to the States for the latter half.

Since I was young, I've always had a passion for building and creating. At 7, I'd spend hours building things out of Lego. As I grew a litte bit older, I continued to explore different forms of art, including drawing my own comic books at age 11 and sharing them with my friends. It wasn't until I was 15 that I discovered my love for design, after teaching myself how to use photography and filmmaking software like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro. To me, these different mediums are all just tools to help me tell a story, convey a message, and make a connection with others. I owe my passion for design and user experience to these early experiences with art and creativity.
