About

For the last 5 years, I've been crafting meaningful design solutions from consumer experiences to enterprise softwares. I enjoy problem-solving with a goal-driven and data-informed approach, and balancing the needs of the end-user and business goals. I love that I get to do this by collaborating and building partnerships with teams from product and engineering to customer success and business development.

I was born in Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City), and I spent half my life in Vietnam and the other half in the States. At 15, I discovered design when I began teaching myself photography and filmmaking. I owe my passion for design and user experience to these arts. For me, they're merely different mediums used to convey a story, a message, and making a connection.
