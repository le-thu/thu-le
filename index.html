About

I find joy in design exploration, crafting meaningful digital experiences, and problem solving through a goal-driven and data-informed approach. I love that I get to do this by being a collaborator working with many folks from product and engineering to customer success.

After spending the first half of my life in Vietnam and the other half in the States, I'm now once again based in Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City), Vietnam — my birth city and one that holds a special place in my heart.

I stumbled upon design when I started to tinker with photography and filmmaking around the age of 15, which I will always give credit to for my passion for design and user experience. To me, they're merely different mediums used to tell a story, a message, a connection.
