About

For the last 5 years, I've been crafting meaningful design solutions from consumer experiences to enterprise softwares. I enjoy problem-solving with a goal-driven and data-informed approach, and balancing the needs of the end-user and business goals. I love that I get to do this by collaborating and building partnerships with teams from product and engineering to customer success and business development.

Previously, I designed e-commerce websites at SAI Digital and led product marketing design at crypto exchange Liquid.

I'm currently based in Saigon, Vietnam, where I was born and spent the first half my life before spending the other half in the States. At 15, I discovered design when I began teaching myself photography and filmmaking using Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro. I'd say I owe my passion for design and user experience to these arts. For me, they're merely different mediums used to convey a story, a message, and making a connection.
